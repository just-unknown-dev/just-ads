## 0.1.0

* Initial release.
* Banner, Interstitial, Rewarded, and App Open ad support via `google_mobile_ads`.
* GDPR/UMP consent flow managed by `UmpManager`.
* Unified `JustAdsManager` entry point for initializing and coordinating all ad types.
* `JustAdsConfig` for supplying ad unit IDs and configuration options.
* Ad inspector support via `AdInspector`.
